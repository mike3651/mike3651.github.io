Deriving the geometric series

  • $$Let\hspace{0.25cm}S = \sum_{i=0}^{\infty}a^i = a^0 + a^1 + a^2 + ....$$
  • We multiply S by a to get \(a*(a^0 + a^1 + a^2 + ....) = a + a^2 + ....\)
  • Now we subtract aS from S to get 1 \(\rightarrow S*(1-a) = 1 \rightarrow s = \frac{1}{1-a}\)
  • This implies that $$S = \sum_{i=0}^{\infty}a^i = \frac{1}{1-a}$$
