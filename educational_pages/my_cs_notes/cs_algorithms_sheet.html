Master Theorem

Helpful theorem for quickly finding the bounds of recurrences in the form \(aT(\frac{n}{b}) + f(n^d)\)

Theorem:

  • \(T(n) \in O(n^d) \hspace{1.4cm} d > \lg_ba\)
  • \(T(n) \in O(n^d\lg_2n)\hspace{0.5cm} d = \lg_ba\)
  • \(T(n) \in O(n^{\lg_ba})\hspace{1.0cm} d < \lg_ba\)
